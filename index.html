FAQ

Common
questions.

Can't find your answer? Contact us

How do I install the booking widget on my website? +
We provide a single embed code — paste it into your website HTML where you want the booking form to appear. Works with WordPress, Squarespace, Webflow, and custom sites. Setup takes under 5 minutes.
Can I try it before paying? +
Yes. All plans include a full 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You get access to every feature in your chosen plan so you can evaluate everything before committing.
What payment methods can customers use to book? +
Profi and Expert plans support online card payments via Stripe. Customers pay securely on your website at booking time. Stripe processing fees (approx. 1.4% + €0.25 for European cards) apply separately.
Can I switch plans later? +
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the end of your current billing period.
Do you support schools with multiple locations? +
Yes — the Expert plan supports multiple branches from a single account. Each location has its own booking widget, instructor pool, and reporting, all manageable from one dashboard.
Is support available during peak ski season? +
All plans include email support. The Expert plan includes priority support 7 days a week including weekends — exactly when you need it most during high season.
